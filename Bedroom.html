BEDROOM

Japandi Style

Combines elements of Japanese minimalism with Scandinavian design. It's characterized by clean lines, neutral colors, and the use of natural materials like wood and simple, functional furniture.

Boho Style

Boho style, short for bohemian, is eclectic and free-spirited. It features a mix of patterns, textures, and colors, often with a focus on global and vintage decor elements.

Rustic Style

Rustic bedrooms have a warm and cozy feel. They often feature natural materials like wood and stone, distressed finishes, and a connection to nature through decor.

American Style

Modern American style is characterized by clean lines, neutral color palettes, and a blend of modern and traditional elements to create a timeless, comfortable space.

Masculine Style

This features dark and bold color schemes, simple and functional furniture, and minimalistic decor.

Small Apartment

This bedroom focus on space-saving solutions and multi-functional furniture to maximize the use of limited space.

Nursery Room

Designed for infants and young children. This features soft colors, baby-friendly furniture, and playful decor.

Shared Bedroom

This often incorporate bunk beds, storage solutions, and a balance of decor elements suitable for different ages and genders.

Loft Style

Typically located in urban loft spaces. This features open layouts, high ceilings, industrial elements, and a blend of modern and vintage decor.

Industrial Style

Embrace a raw and urban aesthetic with features like exposed brick walls, metal accents, concrete floors, and utilitarian furniture.

Hotel room style

Aim for a clean, luxurious, and comfortable ambiance with high-quality linens, elegant decor, and attention to detail.

Wabi Sabi Style

A Japanese concept that appreciates the beauty in imperfections. Bedrooms in this style focus on simplicity, natural materials, and a sense of humility.
