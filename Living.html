LIVING ROOM AREA

Wabi Sabi Style

A Wabi Sabi style living room embodies simplicity, minimalism, and a sense of quiet beauty.

Rustic Style

A rustic style living room embraces a warm, cozy, and down-to-earth aesthetic, often drawing inspiration from rural and countryside settings.

American Style

A classic American style living room is characterized by a timeless and traditional design that draws inspiration from various periods of American interior design history. It combines elements of elegance, comfort, and sophistication.

Minimalist Style

Decor is kept to a minimum. Select a few key decor items or artwork pieces that align with the minimalist aesthetic. A single, striking piece of art can serve as a focal point.

Industrial Style

Industrial living rooms typically feature a neutral color palette, including grays, blacks, and shades of brown. These colors create a neutral backdrop for industrial decor.

Small Apartment

Opt for small-scale furniture pieces that fit the space well. Avoid bulky or oversized furniture that can make the room feel cramped.

Indian Style

Indian living rooms often feature a rich and vibrant color palette, including deep reds, bright oranges, royal blues, and earthy tones. Traditional Indian rugs and carpets, like dhurries and kilims, are often used to add color and texture to the floor.

Boho Style

Boho living rooms often feature an abundance of indoor plants, such as hanging macram√© planters and potted foliage. Plants add a natural and vibrant element.

Man-cave Style

A man cave living room is a dedicated space within a home designed for relaxation, entertainment, and personal enjoyment. It's often a place where the man of the house can pursue his hobbies, interests, or simply unwind in a space that reflects his personality.

Spanish-Colonial Style

A Spanish Colonial style living room is characterized by its warm and inviting ambiance, a mix of rustic and ornate details, and a strong sense of history and culture.

Tropical living room style

Decorate with botanical prints, palm leaf patterns, and floral motifs on upholstery, curtains, and throw pillows. Rattan and wicker furniture, such as chairs, coffee tables, and dining sets, contribute to the tropical aesthetic.

Outdoor Style

An outdoor living area is an extension of your indoor living space designed for relaxation, entertainment, and enjoyment of the outdoors. Whether you have a spacious backyard, a small patio, or a rooftop deck, creating an outdoor living area can enhance your quality of life and allow you to make the most of your outdoor space.
