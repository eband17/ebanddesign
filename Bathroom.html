Modern Style

Rustic Style

Typically features natural wood, stone, and distressed finishes, along with elements like reclaimed barn wood and vintage fixtures.

Industrial Style

Feature exposed brick walls, metal accents, and concrete floors. Vintage plumbing fixtures and open shelving are common in this style.

Japanese Zen Style

It includes natural materials like wood and stone, as well as minimalistic design elements for a serene ambiance.

Coastal Style

They often use light colors, sea-inspired decor, and natural materials like driftwood and wicker to create a relaxed, seaside feel.

Scandinavian Style

It emphasizes simplicity, with a focus on natural light and minimalist design.

Rustic Style

Rustic bathrooms have a cozy, countryside charm.

Eclectic Style

Eclectic bathrooms blend various design elements, mixing different styles, colors, and patterns for a unique and personalized look.

Glamorous Style

Glamorous bathrooms emphasize luxury and opulence. They often include ornate fixtures, dramatic lighting, and high-end materials like marble and crystal.

Minimalist Style

Minimalist bathrooms focus on simplicity and decluttered spaces. They often feature sleek, streamlined fixtures, neutral colors, and a reduction of unnecessary decor items.

Contemporary-style

Unique lighting fixtures and innovative storage solutions. They often blend various materials and textures for a distinct look.
